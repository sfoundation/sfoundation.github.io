Second batch of students selected



The second batch of students for the Each One Teach One program have been selected!

The second batch of students have been selected after the test was conducted at Savitree Foundation Office, Champati on 27 March, 2016, amongst 52 students from 11 schools around Padmapur region in Cuttack. After the IQ test, clinical psychologists personally met and evaluated each candidate before declaring 9 students who would receive the support.
