Fourth batch of students selected



The fourth batch of students for the Each One Teach One program have been selected!

The fourth batch of students have been selected after the test was conducted at Savitree Foundation Office, Champati on 25 March, 2018, amongst 90 students from schools around Padmapur region in Cuttack in two batches. After the IQ test, clinical psychologists personally met and evaluated each candidate before declaring 7 students who would receive the support.
