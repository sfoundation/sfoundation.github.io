Healthcare Program



The details for the healthcare program.

Healthcare facilities in the state still needs a lot of improvement. Through our tie up with one of the best teaching hospitals of the state i.e. IMS & SUM Hospital, we have decided to provide specialized healthcare to the residents of Champati and surrounding villages. Doctors from specialized and super specialized wings like Medicine, Opthalmology, Paediatrics, Pulmonary Medicine< Neurology, Gastroenterology, Oncology etc shall camp at the foundation office at regular intervals and provide free check up facility and medicines to the patients.

We conducted a mega health camp, which helped us assess the medical requirements of the residents of the area. The specialist doctor visits are planned accordingly.
