A health camp was conducted on 29 March 2015 at Padmapur high school, champati in collaboration with IMS & SUM hospital. Free basic health checkup, speciality checkups for eye, ENT, dental, ECG, blood sugar and dermatology and super-speciality checkups in gastroenterology and cardiology was provided alongwith free dispersal of required medicine. A total of 612 people benefited from this program.
