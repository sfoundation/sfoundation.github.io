We strive to evolve as an exemplary non-profit organization for all round development of rural Odisha.

Our work specifically focuses on the Champati region of Cuttack. We also work on encouraging many more individuals/groups to setup similar organizations in other parts of rural Odisha.

Record rice grain yield due to scientific cropping

Scientific cropping methods and agricultural implements provided by Savitree Foundation to certain farmers in Champati Village have resulted in highest ever per area yield of paddy in the region (decided by the State Department of Agriculture.). The associated scientists have prepared a document for the same.

Intensive Agricultural Development Programme Meeting

Experts from different fields of Agriculture of IAS, SOA University and Coordinator CRTD, SOA University met the local farmers and explored the possibility of farm mechanization and suitability for crop diversification in the agricultural land neighboring the Savitree Foundation Training center. A report and a presentation was made for the same.
