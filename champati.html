The Champati Project

Champati is a village situated in Jagatpur tehsil in Cuttack district of Odisha. Famous for its high quality fireworks, this small village lies in the serene East Odisha countryside. Our aim lies in establishing a model society in Champati and its holistic development. The following work has been done already:

Academic support for bright students

Every year from 2015 onwards, bright students from economically backward families are selected on the basis of their performance in a unique IQ test devised and performed by certified clinical psychologists. The foundation will bear the entire educational expenditure for these students, including special coaching till they find their own feet in the society.

Health camp

A health camp was conducted on 29 Mar '15 at Padmapur high school, champati in collaboration with IMS & SUM hospital. Free basic health checkup, speciality checkups for eye, ENT, dental, ECG, blood sugar and dermatology and super-speciality checkups in gastroenterology and cardiology was provided alongwith free dispersal of required medicine.
