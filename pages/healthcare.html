Healthcare

The foundation strives to extend cheap healthcare services to ensure proper health maintenance amongst the under privileged. We intend to offer free health camps and speciality services on a periodic basis at various locations starting at our base project area at Champati.

Health Camp on 29 March

A health camp was conducted on 29 Mar '15 at Padmapur high school, champati in collaboration with IMS & SUM hospital. Free basic health checkup, speciality checkups for eye, ENT, dental, ECG, blood sugar and dermatology and super-speciality checkups in gastroenterology and cardiology was provided alongwith free dispersal of required medicine. A total of 612 people benefited from this program.
